Ubuntu African Safaris

For four generations, our family has welcomed hunters to experience the magic of the African bush. Situated in the renowned Kalahari region of South Africa's North West province, Ubuntu African Safaris offers world-class hunting experiences combined with unparalleled luxury accommodations.

Our 50,000-acre private reserve is home to exceptional populations of lion, plains game, and dangerous game, all thriving in their natural habitat. We take pride in ethical, sustainable hunting practices that contribute to conservation and community development.
